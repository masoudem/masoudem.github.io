About:

Over the 2-years past, I have been a laboratory instructor and head of hardware and software labs at the Department of Computer Engineering at the Amirkabir University of Technology. I have improved my experience with sensors and microcontrollers such as FPGA, Arduino, Arm, and a wide range of sensors.

Also, I am highly skilled back-end and Machine Learning developer with a strong foundation in electrical engineering. I just graduated with my M.S.C degree in Electrical Engineering (Mechatronic and Robotic) at the Amirkabir University of Technology, advised by Prof. Jafar MiliMonfared, working on Statistics, Machine Learning, Ensemble methods, Smart Monitoring, IoT, and Renewable Energy. I am also a team player and have a proven track record of working effectively with others. I am passionate about using my skills to solve real-world problems, and I am always eager to learn and grow.

Research

My research interests lie in the intersection of Renewable Energy, Machine learning, deep learning, Smart Grid, Monitoring systems, IoT, sensors, and Autonomous systems. I am particularly interested in using these technologies to develop new and innovative solutions for a variety of problems.

Design and implementation an smart monitoring system in photovoltaic power plants based on IoT (2017 - 2019)

Developed a new optical controller for robotic manipulation based on IoRT (Internet of Robotics Things) (2018 - 2019)

Develop a Robust Optimal Adaptive Sliding Mode Control with Disturbance Observer for a SCARA Robot System (2017 – 2018)

Design a smart meter using a field-programmable gate array (FPGA) to monitor body health (2020-2021)
